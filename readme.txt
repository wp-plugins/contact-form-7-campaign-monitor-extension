Simple way to integrate Contact Form 7 and Campaign Monitor.

== Description ==

Integrate Contact Form 7 with Campaign Monitor. Automatically add form submissions to predetermined lists in Campaign Monitor, using its latest API.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `contact-form-7-cm-ext` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Campaign Monitor: Subscriber List Details' menu in your contact from 7 editor.

For basic usage, you can also have a look at the [plugin homepage](http://contactform7.com/).
